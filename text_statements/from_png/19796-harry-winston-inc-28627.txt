As a member of the RJC, we are independently audited against the Responsible Jewelry Code of Practices an international
standard on responsible business practices for diamonds, gold and platinum group metals. The Code of Practices addresses
human rights, employment and labor standards, environmental impact, mining practices, product disclosure, and other relevant
topics in the jewelry supply chain. We require all employees who work on supply chain management to understand the RJC
Code of Practices. Employees are trained to investigate violations of the Code of Practices and take appropriate action California Transparency in Supply Chains

steps to address the situation. Specifically, the RJC provides to Harry Winston Third-party verification of Harry Winston's
supply chain and certification that Harry Winston adheres to all of the standards in its Code of Practices, which includes issues of
human trafficking and slavery. Independently, outside of the RJC requirements, Harry Winston undertakes to require suppliers
