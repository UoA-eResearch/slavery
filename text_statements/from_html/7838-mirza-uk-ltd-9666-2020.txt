***** MODERN SLAVERY ACT 2015: SLAVERY AND HUMAN TRAFFICKING STATEMENT *****
**** INTRODUCTION FROM ANWARUL HABIB, DIRECTOR ****
We are proud of the steps we have taken to seek to combat slavery and human
trafficking and are committed to improving our practices to seek to combat
slavery and human trafficking.
**** Organisation’s Structure ****
We are a wholesale supplier of clothing and footwear.
**** Our Policies on slavery and human trafficking ****
We are committed to seeking that there is no modern slavery or human
trafficking in our supply chains or in any part of our business.Â  Our policy
on anti-slavery reflects our commitment to acting ethically and with integrity
in all our business relationships and to implementing and enforcing effective
systems and controls to seek to ensure that slavery and human trafficking is
not taking place anywhere in our supply chains.

As part of our policy to seek to identify and mitigate risk we have in place
systems to seek to:
    * Identify and assess potential risk areas in our supply chains in respect

    * Mitigate the risk of slavery and human trafficking occurring in our

    * Monitor potential risk areas in our supply chains.
    * Protect whistle blowers.
**** Training ****
To ensure a sufficient level of understanding of the risks of modern slavery
and human trafficking in our supply chains and our business, we provide
training to our staff where necessary.
