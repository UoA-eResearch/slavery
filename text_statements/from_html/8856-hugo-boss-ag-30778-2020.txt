**** Verification of Product Supply Chain ****
Prior to the start of a business relationship every direct supplier is required
to sign and acknowledge the HUGO BOSS Social Standards which include provisions
on the prohibition of trafficking and slavery.
**** Compliance audits ****
On a regular basis our direct suppliers are required to undergo compliance
audits which include checks for compliance with the prohibition of trafficking
and slavery. Audits are conducted by internal or external partners and
scheduled together with the suppliers.
**** Certification of supply chain security ****
The HUGO BOSS General Terms and Conditions obligate suppliers to comply with
the HUGO BOSS Social Standards and ensure compliance through their supply
chain.
**** Internal accountability ****
If suppliers violate our HUGO BOSS Social Standards we issue an action plan,
requesting them to improve compliance with our standards. The supplierâs
performance will be reviewed in a follow up audit. If a supplier fails to make
progress against the plan, he may be subject to sanctions up to and including
termination of the business relationship.
**** Internal trainings ****
All our employees undergo annual Social Standards trainings. Our internal and
external audit staff consists of experienced and well trained auditors.
